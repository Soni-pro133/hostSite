Sai Krushna
Arogya Dham (SKAD)

"Sai Krushna Arogya Dham (SKAD)" - a free primary healthcare center run by the Trust that uniquely synthesizes the needs of the body and the mind. SKAD which opened in March 2017 has served nearly 2000 patients since its inauguration. Most patients visit from about 10 villages nearby and find it a huge boon in the absence of any nearby medical facilities in a 5 km radius. The Sai Krushna Arogya Dham provides free consultation, free medicine and free treatment for all patients irrespective of their financial background.

"Most patients come with some common ailments like eye issues, dental problems and skin disorders. I also try to analyze the patient data to evolve patterns. For instance, in the months of Feb-March, we see a specific proclivity among the villagers to use hot water which causes increased dryness and skin rashes. Likewise, many villagers do not brush their teeth properly leading to dental concerns. Targeting such trends, we came up with awareness campaigns which we drive through the school students in their respective villages. All consultation and treatment is provided completely free of cost"

Dr Poornima Cartic Medical Officer at Sai Krushna Arogyadham
